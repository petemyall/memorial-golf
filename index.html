⛳ Annual Golf Day at Billingbear Golf Club

🗓️ Date: Sunday 27th July

⏰ Tee Time: 9:40am sharp

📍 Venue: Billingbear Golf Club – Old & New Courses

👥 Players: Dave Jnr, Jack, Rich, Steve, Pete, Geoff, Jim, and Wayne

We’ll be heading out in two four-balls to play 9 holes on the Old Course first. After the morning round, we’ll stop at the clubhouse for a well-earned pint and some food.
It’ll be the perfect chance to refuel, share a few laughs, and recharge before heading back out.

Then it’s back out for another 9 holes on the New Course to round off the day. Expect plenty of laughs, a few heroic shots (and a few not-so-heroic ones), and a great day of golf with good mates.
